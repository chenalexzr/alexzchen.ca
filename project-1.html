Genshin Impact Tribute

Genshin Impact is an action role-playing game developed and published by Chinese video game development and animation studio miHoYo.

The following paintings listed here are personal studies conducted using characters from Genshin Impact as a fun exercise



Alex Chen is a freelance Illustrator and Designer based in Vancouver, British Columbia.

Brimming with curiosity and a love for painting, you can always expect to see him working on a brand new project to satisfy his drawing mind.
