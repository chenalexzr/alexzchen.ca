Blue Archive Inspired Character Design

Blue Archive is an anime RPG mobile game developed by Korean studio NAT games. The following game has "gacha" related mechanics and takes place in the academic city of Kivotos. You play as an advisor from administrative group "Schale" and interact with students across Kivotos from different schools.

I designed my character as a student of the "Millennium Science School"; A campus solely focused on cutting-edge technology and sciences. The equipment, inventions, and infrastructure across Kivotos are all influenced by the Millennium School of Science.
