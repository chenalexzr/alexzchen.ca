VirtuaREAL Album Redesign

The VirtuaREAL Project created by ©USAGI Production is a music album series focused on collaborating with Japanese online personalities (also known as "Virtual Youtubers") and music producers. ©USAGI Production's goal is to create new forms of entertainment, cultivating the next generation of culture while utilizing the idea "free expression".

The following are unofficial cover redesigns I did as a 3-day intensive side-project for the album VirtuaREAL BEST.01 which include the following:

  • VirtuaREAL BEST .01 Album

  • DISC 01: BEST SELECTIONS
  • DISC 02: REMIXES
  • DISC 01 BEST SELECTIONS Single Track no 6. 天使であれたら / 白百合リリィ
  • DISC 02: REMIXES Single Track no 21. アザミナ (K's Remix) / ロボ子さん
