If given the chance I'd challenge Da Vinci and Michelangelo to a drawing battle. I'd still win even if I were to lose.

I'm Illustrator and Designer born and raised in Vancouver, BC. Most recently I graduated from Capilano University's IDEA Program in 2022.

With a wide range of interests from anime, manga, comic books, dramas, music, and video games; I'll pretty much love anything that tells a good story and makes the gears in my brain spin. I can also speak 3 languages: Cantonese, English, Japanese, and slowly but surely Korean.

New technologies always get me excited too, I'm all over things that allow me to be more efficient; As a result of this I spend my personal time mastering a multitude of the Adobe Suite. My other interests also include frequently tinkering around with computers and even building my own workstation from scratch.

Feel free to contact me at nattsumeillust@gmail.com if you're interested ;)
