If given the chance I'd challenge Da Vinci and Michelangelo to a drawing battle. I mean you only live once, so why not live it to the extreme?

An Illustrator and Designer born and raised in Vancouver, BC. I have a wide range of interests from anime, manga, comic books, dramas, music, and video games; I'll pretty much love anything that tells a good story and makes the gears in my brain spin. I can also speak 3 languages: Cantonese, English, Japanese, and slowly but surely Korean.

New technologies always get me excited too, anything that screams efficiency gets me going. As a result of this I spend my personal time developing my proficiency using a multitude of Adobe products and even miscellanious things like building my own PC from scratch.
